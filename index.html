Get Started

How and what can i do on Skim&Scan?
Share Notes

On Skim&Scan you can upload notes(preferably Engineering related content) that can be useful to a large number of students belonging to the VTU(Vishveshwaraya Technological University).The aim is to make a reliable archive that depends on the works of students and teachers.

Download Content

From the Downloads section you can download content that is relevant to your course. The downloads could be in the form of PDF's, E-PUB's, and also referal links to other websites.

Access a wide range of study material

On Skim&Scan you can access a wide range of study material for 1-8 semesters of all branches of engineering that includes previous year question papers, question banks and even hand written notes from professors and lecturers.

Features.Skim&Scan has the following features to offer.

Skim&Scan helps users find all content related to engineering disicplines for all semesters which includes guides, text books, pdf notes and previous question papers.It also includes e-books,and e-journals and a wide range of learning material.

Knowldege Sharing

Using Skim&Scan one can access academic material that is created by a community of students, teachers and professors. The content can be uploaded by using the upload link. The aim is to build a well organized archive of materials that is suitable for VTU syllabus needs.

Contribute

Any user can contribute by simply uploading his/her works so that it is available for download by other users.Any problem that occurs with content such as originality of content or errors can be reported.The authenticity of the content can up or down voted depending on various factors.

Additional Features

Users can upload their own content and the popularity depends on the numbe r of upvotes or down votes. Scanners and Exam Guides are also a part of the content that is being shared. Soft copies of text books are available for download.VTU syllabus and Lab materials for all disciplines are available.
